# Roadmap: Neurovia

## Phase 1 — Trustworthy Italy MVP

Deliver the complete browser-based discovery loop: bilingual center directory, synchronized interactive map/list, verified source links, clinic detail interface, and moderated review/center-submission prototypes.

## Phase 2 — Trust and Moderation Backend

Add verified accounts, secure persistence, anti-abuse controls, moderation queues, audit trails, center responses, and production Bayesian scoring.

## Phase 3 — European Expansion

Add country-aware access metadata, translation workflow, European center ingestion, cross-border filters, and regional governance.
