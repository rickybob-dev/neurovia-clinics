# Project Research Summary

## Key Findings

- Italian neurological rehabilitation providers differ materially by inpatient/outpatient mode, target condition, age group, SSN access, and specialist technology; a single unexplained ranking hides these distinctions.
- Official institutional pages can support specialties, technologies, address, and care-mode claims, but not comparative quality claims.
- Review systems in health contexts need relationship declarations, moderation, privacy controls, provider response rights, and resistance to small-sample ranking distortion.
- A synchronized list/map supports geographical discovery, while the detail layer must keep verified facts separate from community reports.

## Implications for Roadmap

- Ship verified-fact discovery before production scoring.
- Treat authentication, moderation, and scoring as a security-sensitive backend phase.
- Design country and language as record attributes now to avoid rebuilding during European expansion.
